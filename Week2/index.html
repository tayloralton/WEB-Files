Everything You Need to Know About Taylor Swift!

About Taylor

Taylor Swift was born on December 13th, 1989 in Reading, PA. She has a brother named Austin who is two years younger. She moved to Nashville when she was 14 years old to pursue her music career. She got her first intern record deal with RCA Records but walked away from it after a year. She was discovered in the BlueBird cafe and started a record label with the person who discovered her. Her first album is self-titled, Taylor Swift. She is now the music industry after having nine albums in 3 different categories of music.

Her Music

Her best album is Folklore. Her best song is All Too Well. Her best concert was Speak Now or Reputation.
